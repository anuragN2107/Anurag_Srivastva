## About Me

I work across the data stack — from moving raw data through pipelines to putting a working model or dashboard in front of someone who has to make a decision with it. Most of that work runs through SQL, Python, and PySpark, with Snowflake and Power BI on the reporting side and Airflow keeping the scheduled jobs honest.

My current focus is bringing machine learning and generative AI closer to production: forecasting models that hold up outside a notebook, and GenAI features that solve an actual workflow problem rather than a demo.

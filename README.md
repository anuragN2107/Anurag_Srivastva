## <img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/hvRJCLFzcasrR4ia7z/giphy.gif" width="30"> About Me

<img src="https://capsule-render.vercel.app/api?type=rect&color=0:0D1117,100:0D1117&height=55&section=header&text=%3E%20whoami&fontColor=39FF14&fontSize=20&fontAlign=5" width="100%"/>

I work at the intersection of data engineering and applied AI — the part of the stack where raw, messy data turns into something a business can actually act on. My day-to-day runs across SQL, Python, and PySpark, with a growing focus on wiring machine learning and generative AI into real pipelines instead of leaving them as notebooks nobody touches again.

I like building things end to end: pulling data from a source, cleaning it, modeling it, and putting it in front of someone who has to make a decision with it. Snowflake and Power BI show up a lot in that chain, along with Airflow when things need to run on their own at 4 AM without me watching.

Lately I've been spending more time on the GenAI side — prompt engineering, NLP work, and figuring out where large language models actually add value versus where they're just noise. Still learning, still shipping, still breaking things in staging before they break in production.
